There are many ways to go about ordering food online. I chose to go with a simple drop down menu for the items on their lunch specials. for regular lunch menu items you have to option to choose the entree and then the side. After choosing you can add it straight to your cart. Then for their specialty lunch options I gave them their own section because they do not come with a side.


This design improves the user experience of the survey for a variety of reasons. First of all, it automatically provides grade percentage options based on the grade letter inputted. Second, it utilizes constraints that do not allow a student to input an answer that is not logical, which could skew the survey results. Lastly, the design is easier to understand, and it more closely follows the typical grading scale used at UNA. These factors will greatly improve the expereince for users.
